Falafel (طعمية)

Total Time: 1 hr
Difficulty: ★★

Ingredients:
  • Dried Halved Fava Beans
  • Spring Onions
  • Parsley
  • Dill
  • Yellow Onions
  • Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Ground dried cayenne chili

Directions:
  1. First rehydrate the fava beans for a few days beforehand changing the water daily.
  2. In a food processor, begin putting all the fava beans until they are blended into a course paste. Next, add the onions, spring onions, parsley, and dill until it is a paste. Add all of it to a large bowl. Next add salt, cumin, coriander, pepper, and dried crushed cayanne pepper. Add these spices to taste.
  3. To cook, add enough neutral oil into a pan to shallow fry. Form the mixture into small patties with sesame seeds sprinkled on both sides. Cook each side for a few minutes or until they are dark brown. Then flip. Once it is crispy on both sides, take it out on a plate with paper towels to soak up the excess oil. Serve with tahini, pita, and salad!
