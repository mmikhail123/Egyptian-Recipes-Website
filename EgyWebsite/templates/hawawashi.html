Hawawashi (حواوشي)

Total Time: 1 hr 20 min
Difficulty: ★

Ingredients:
  • 1lb of ground meat (beef or turkey)
  • Pita Bread
  • Melted Butter
  • 1 can crushed tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • Healthy amount of parsley
  • 4 Bay Leaves
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Healthy amount of Chili Powder
  • Healthy amount of Paprika
  • Healthy amount of Garlic Powder
  • Healthy amount of Dried Herbs
  • Healthy amount of Dried Chilis (guajillo, ancho, or cayenne)
  • Healthy amount of Cumin

Directions:
  1. Start by making the ground meat layer. Start by finely dicing one onion and a few cloves of garlic. In a pan, heat natural oil. Once hot, add the onions/garlic along wiht a few chilis and the bay leaves. Season with a generous amount of salt and pepper to taste. Once the onions sweat and become translucent, add the ground meat. Add the rest of the seasonings to taste. Once the ground meat has been browned and little liquid remains, add the tomato paste until it has been fully cooked and incorporated in the mixture. Then add the can of crushed tomato sauce into the pan. Then add the parsley and adjust the seasonings to taste. Let it simmer on low-medium heat for 15 min. Feel free to add bits of water if dry (but not too much!).
  2. Now cut your pita into halves and open them. Stuff a healthy amount into each pocket. Layer them on a pan to put into the oven. Brush both side of the pita with butter.
  3. Heat the oven to broil to 450 degrees. Once heated, put the hawawashi into the oven, flipping once one side has been browned. Each side should take 5-10 min (but keep an eye on them!).
  4. SERVE!
