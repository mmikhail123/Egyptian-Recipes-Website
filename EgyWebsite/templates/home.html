Koshary (كشري)

The national dish of Egypt. A dish containing a mix of pasta, lentils, and rice topped off with a vinager tomato sauce and fried onions.

Molokhia (ملوخيه)

Meat stew made with finely chopped mallow leaves.

Macarona Bechamel (مكرونة بشاميل)

Layered pasta dish of penne, meat ragu, and bechamel sauce.

Falafel (طعمية)

Fried spiced fava bean fritter that is typically served in pita with tahini and salad.

Goulash (جلاش باللحمة)

Phyllo pie with spiced meat.

Hawawashi (حواوشي)

Stuffed pita with spiced ground meat.

Kofta (كفتة اللحم)

Grilled spiced meat that is usually served with tahini, salad, and pita.

Mahshi (محشي)

Stuffed vegetables with a spiced rice and ground meat filling.

Mombar (ممبار)

A type of Egyptian sausage of stuffed beef intenstines with spiced rice and meat.
